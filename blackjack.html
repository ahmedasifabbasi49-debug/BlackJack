Game Rules
Casino Royale Blackjack
The Basics
Objective
Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over
Blackjack
Ace + any 10-value card on the first deal pays 3 to 2
Bust
Going over 21 — you automatically lose the hand
Push
Tie with the dealer — your full bet is returned
Card Values
2 – 10
Worth their face value
J, Q, K
Worth 10 each
Ace
Worth 1 or 11 — whichever helps your hand more
Player Actions
Hit
Take another card from the dealer
Stand
Keep your hand and end your turn
Double Down
Double your bet — receive exactly one more card, then automatically stand
Split
Split any matching pair into two separate hands (up to 4 hands) — each gets its own bet
Insurance
When dealer shows an Ace, bet up to half your wager that they have Blackjack — pays 2 to 1
Dealer Rules
Must Hit
Dealer hits on soft 17 (Ace + 6) and any total below 17
Must Stand
Dealer stands on hard 17 or higher
Shoe
6 decks — reshuffled automatically when the shoe runs low
